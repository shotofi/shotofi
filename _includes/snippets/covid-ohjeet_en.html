Covid-19 instructions autumn 2021

You're allowed to train, if you have received two vaccinations or immunity after recovering from covid-19. We require trainees to have a valid vaccination certificate, when there are restrictions to gatherings.

  • * Training is forbidden, if you or someone close to you have any symptoms.
  • * We monitor the epidemic situation and adapt our training accordingly.
  • * Generic instructions:
  • - Wash your hands carefully with soap and warm water before and after training.
  • - Cover sneezing and coughs with sleeve.
  • - Use mask in dressing room and other crowded areas.
  • - Mask recommendation is valid as long as the public recommendation on using them.
  • * In case of a suspected infection in a training group, general recommendations on quarantine apply.
  • * Instuctor will keep note on who is present every training session.
  • * If possible, ventilate the dojo before session.
  • * Dojo is cleaned according to instructions after every training session.
