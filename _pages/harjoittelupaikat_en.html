TRAINING PLACES

Kaapelitehdas

Tallberginkatu 1, Helsinki

Enter from staircase D, dressing rooms are on the 3rd floor, up one floor turn right and again right at the end of a long hallway to get to the practice hall. The practice hall belongs to Aikido Club Awase.

Lockers in changing rooms do not have locks, so it is advised to bring your own padlock.

Otahalli

Otaranta 6, Espoo

Practice takes place in Aava exercise hall.

UniDojo - Pohjoinen Hesperiankatu 25

UniDojo will be closed in June 2019, as a new gym will be opened in its place.
